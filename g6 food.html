What is Lanao del Sur known for?
Lanao's rich and fertile soil provides fruitful harvest for their agricultural industry. Rice is their main crop followed by corn, coconut, abaca, bananas and durian. Brass making and malong-weaving are some of their cottage industries. The province's main attractions are mostly concentrated in Marawi City.
